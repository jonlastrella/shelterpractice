Become a Friend!

We need your help, and every little bit makes a huge difference to the animals in need.

BECOME A FRIEND♥

One of the best ways you can help an animal is by joining with other compassionate animal lovers as a Friend of the Humane Society for Tacoma and Pierce County. Friends help ensure that every animal receives protection, compassion, love, and care. Your monthly donation helps us continue our life-saving work of finding homes, providing food, emergency medical care, and shelter for animals in our community who need us.

With your monthly support, every animal can have a bright and loving future! Please give desperate animals the second chance they deserve.

We Are Blown Away By Our Community!

Recently, we asked for your help in raising the funds needed to replace our failing X-ray machine, and you answered our call in a big way.

Because of you, we were able to raise more than $37,936 to cover the cost of this vital piece of equipment in just three days. WOW!

Taking x-rays is critically important for us to diagnose animals and provide them with the specialized care they need. Just like Rainer, who recovered well from his amputation surgery and was adopted over the weekend. This lucky boy will now live a pain-free life in his new forever home because of your support!
